My Top-3 Movies Of All-Time

Hacksaw Ridge

MovieYear: 2016
ActorName: Andrew Garfield

World War II American Army Medic Desmond T. Doss,
serving during the Battle of Okinawa,
refuses to kill people and becomes the first man in American history to receive the Medal of Honor without firing a shot.

American History X

MovieYear: 1998
ActorName: Edward Norton

Living a life marked by violence, neo-Nazi Derek finally goes to prison after killing two black youths.
Upon his release, Derek vows to change; he hopes to prevent his brother, Danny, who idolizes Derek, from following in his footsteps.
